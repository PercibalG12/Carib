• 17th to 20th century

    Development of Caribbean Literature

    Early Caribbean Literature was made in the image of Europeans and indentured immigrants as a result of slavery and indenturdship.

  • 1865+

    More development

    After independence in the Caribbean Literature started to grow. from this growth diffrent visions were expressed of what Literature should represent as it related to our history.

  • 1900's

    Native development

    A Guyanese novelist Edgar Austin Mittelholzer was born and his works influenced a rise in Caribbean Literature, earning him the title of a major pioneer in the field of Literature for the Caribbean.

  • 1920-1930

    Take over the world

    Caribbean Literature influence spread around the world, most notably NewYork.As a result of this the Harlem Renaissance began.
